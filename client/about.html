About

This site is an online version of Sid Sackson's Acquire board game. You can create an account (or log in as a guest) and play with other people online.

Gameplay

Gameplay is based on this version of the official rules, with the following exceptions:

  • No special rules are applied to 2-player games.
  • Tiles that become "dead" (would merge safe chains), while in a player's hand are not replaced. If a player draws a dead tile, it is replaced in the manner described in the above rules.

Quitting

A common complaint with multiplayer computer Acquire is quitters. In Acquire there is no resigning, if one player leaves the game abruptly, it ruins the game for the other players, as there is no obvious way to distribute the quitting player's tiles and shares without messing up other players' plans and strategies.

Solution: On this site, games are timed. If a user's time runs out before they make a play, a (dumb) computer player will play for them. This way, if you leave in the middle of a game, the dumb computer will continue to play for you, so you will likely lose, but the other players can continue their game uninterupted.

Chat

You can use the chat box to send messages to other players. While you're in the lobby, your messages are sent to everyone. While you're in a game, your messages are sent to the other players in that game. You can mention other players (type: @example_username my message...) to send them a highlighted message, wherever they are. If you are in a game, you can use @lobby to send a message to the lobby. You can also use @everyone to send a message everywhere on the site. Don't overuse @everyone.

  • Example use of @lobby: @lobby Game5 needs 3 more players!
  • Example use of @everyone: @everyone Don't play with @malicious_user, he wastes time on purpose.
You can also mute other players (type: /mute example_username), to not see their messages. You can /mute everyone to hide all player messages, /mute lobby to not see lobby messages while in a game, or /mute server to hide server messages. Type /unmute example_username to revert the mute.
